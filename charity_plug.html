Shameless Charity Plug

I'm a part of a great organisation called Big Brothers Big Sisters. The organisation matches children from disadvantaged backgrounds that may have lost a parent or have grown up in a broken home. These children are matched with adult mentors who act as friends for the children and spend time with them. These mentors become strong role models for the young mentees and help them develop into confident young adults. If you would like to help this great cause then follow the link below. Thanks.
